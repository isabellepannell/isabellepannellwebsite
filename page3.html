Hagenweise Violins

Why choose a Hagenweise Violin?

Hagenweise violins are one of the best violins for advanced to professional players. They are based on the models of the Stradivarius, which is one of the best violins in the world, and the most rare. The Hagenweise violin is made in Möhrendorf Germany, and the wood is naturally dried and toned by skilled luthiers.

How much does a Hagenweise Violin Cost?

Like the Amadeus violin, the Hagenweise violins' cost will vary depending on the product, however the average cost for the Hagenweise violin in 2020 was $6500.
