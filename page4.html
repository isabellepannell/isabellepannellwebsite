Amadeus Violins

Why choose an Amadeus Violin?

Amadeus Violins are known for their richness in sound, and wonderful projection qualites. The Amadeus violin is beautiful and handmade, with a spruce top lining and maple back. All of the fittings in this violin are ebony, the bridge is from Aubert, France.

How much does an Amadeus Violin Cost?

The Amadeus Violin cost will vary on the product, however the average cost in 2020 for an Amadeus violin was $3600.
